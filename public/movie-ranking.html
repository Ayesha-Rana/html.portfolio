The Best Movies According to Ayesha

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Harry Poerter

Harry Potter is a fictional wizard who embarks on a magical journey of self-discovery, battles dark forces, and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in a beloved fantasy book series by J.K. Rowling.

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse

This animated superhero film is not only visually stunning but also has a compelling story about Miles Morales, a teenager who becomes Spider-Man. It's a great choice for fans of superhero stories.

Drive

Drive is a stylish and intense crime thriller film about a stunt driver who moonlights as a getaway driver, getting entangled in a dangerous criminal underworld.
